NICOLE DAN

Nicole is a third year journalism and political science major at the University of Florida. She is currently a communications intern for Southern Legal Counsel, a public interest law firm in Gainesville, Florida. Throughout her years at UF she has written a column for the Independent Florida Alligator, a student publication, and served as online content editor for Sparks Magazine, an Asian American interest student publcation.
